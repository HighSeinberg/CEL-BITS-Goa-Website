Launchpad
Center for Entrepreneurial Leadership (CEL)'s Exclusive and Curated Community, for the dedicated student entrepreneurs of BITS. Startups who are a part of this community will be given various opportunities and access to special resources along with a wider network to expand their businesses. Creating an environment of mutual learning will help the startups co-exist and solve problems with the help of a larger group of like-minded individuals. As the E-Cell of BITS, it will streamline the process of resource distribution and also assist the startups in their interaction with us. The Startup Community will be given exclusive access to:

  • Resources
  • Community meetups
  • Workshops
  • Mentoring Sessions
IGNITION
A certified two month program, IGNITION offers you the chance to scale and validate your ideas and convert them into reality. Providing the participants with mentors, resources and essential skills like Ideations, Management, Product Development, it is the prefect way to kick start your entrepreneurial career.
CIIE
Centre for Innovation, Incubation and Entrepreneurship (CIIE), at BITS Pilani, Goa Campus aims at creating leaders through inculcating in them an entrepreneurial thinking and thus fostering the spirit of entrepreneurship around us. Nurturing the leaders of tomorrow has always been what the CIIE’s endeavor to achieve. With a mission to inspire campus youth and people around them to lead entrepreneurial innovations at the national and global stage, CIIE maintains a vision to create the right ecosystem for ideas to mature into successful ventures. Freelancer and Co-founder Platform
A platform regulated by CEL, connecting people with brilliant ideas and knowledge together. The platform will serve as a Talent Aggregator, connecting Startups with their potential Interns or Co-Founders.
Rural Entrepreneurship Development
CEL in association with Enactus, BITS Goa has Rural Entrepreneurship Development program, to promote Economic Empowerment with Business Innovation. Through this, CEL also aims to promote self-sustaining Social Ventures, giving back to the Society.
Intern in a StartUp
Intern in a Start-up is an initiative, catering to BITSian Community helping them bag internships at Start-ups. The initiative's motive is to save the fledgling culture of interning at startups by students and help Startups hire the brightest Talents.
Partial Travel Grant
Every year CEL gives a Partial Travel Grant up to 20k to students who represented our College and CEL at Conferences/Business plan competitions. Only selected students get this amount, upon Verification.

Previous Winners are:- Rishikesh Veer, Syed Ubaid Isaaq, Yash Agarwal and Nishchay Mehta.
