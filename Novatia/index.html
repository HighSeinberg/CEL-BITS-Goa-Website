Novatia

The premier B-Plan Competition

"If people are not laughing at your goals, your goals are too small"

- Azim Premji

Novatia is BITS Goa’s flagship B-Plan competition conducted by the Centre for Entrepreneurial Leadership (CEL). It attracts startups and students from all over the country and inspires their very own journey and dream of founding a start up.

Novatia is a premier B-Plan competition held during 2nd February and aims at nurturing the entrepreneurial spirit that can propel one’s idea to become a success. Right from identifying the potential of the idea, providing mentorship by established VC firms, Novatia covers it all.

WHY SHOULD I PARTICIPATE

Novatia 2019 witnessed a participation of 150+ startups. We have successfully associated with VC firms like - Indian Angel Network, Lead Angels, Palaash Ventures etc. in the past editions of Novatia. You definitely don't want to miss this opportunity!

WHO CAN PARTICIPATE

This lucrative opportunity is open for everybody irrespective of whether your startup is in the ideation phase or all the way upto a marketable prototype. Participate in this year's edition of Novatia and win prizes such as huge Cash Amount, mentoring session for a month, Networking, Funding opportunities and much more.

WHEN IS IT GOING TO HAPPEN

Novatia 2020 will be held during Quark, one of India’s largest Techno-Management Fest with a footfall of over 35,000 with a participation of 450+ colleges at BITS-Pilani, K.K Birla Goa Campus
