About Us

"Everyone can tell you the risk, but only an entrepreneur can see the reward‚Äù

Coalescence is BITS Goa's flagship entrepreneurial summit. It aims at providing a platform for individuals with creative ideas and future think tanks, by enabling efficient interaction through informative speaker conferences and stalwart panel discussions. The event attracts major CxO's, budding entrepreneurs , business leaders, industry veterans and venture capitalists across India

In Coalescence, we see an opportunity, a way to achieve what we aim to. To allow an influx of global leaders and facilitate the amalgamation of thought and effort in the entrepreneurial spirit.

Who we are

Centre for Innovation, Incubation and Entrepreneurship at BITS Pilani, Goa Campus aims at creating leaders inculcating in them entrepreneurial thinking and thus fostering the spirit of entrepreneurship around us. We stand for progress.

Our Plan

At CIIE we believe in unlocking the youth's latent inventive potential, by providing hands on experience, to instill and enrich India's entrepreneurial ecosystem. Join us, make Coalescence a part of your journey as an entrepreneur.

Our Vision

With a mission to inspire BITSians and people around them to lead entrepreneurial innovations at the national global stage, CIIE maintains a vision to create the right ecosystem for ideas to mature into successful ventures.
